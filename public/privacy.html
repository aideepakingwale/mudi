01 Information We Collect

MuDi collects only what is necessary to provide the service.

Account information

  • Email address — used to identify your account
  • Display name — shown in rooms to other users
  • Profile picture — shown in the app (from social login, optional)
  • Hashed password — stored for email/password accounts (bcrypt, never plaintext)

Usage information

  • The rooms you create or join (room code, name, timestamps)
  • Last login timestamp
MuDi does not record, store, or transmit your audio files. All file transfers happen directly between your devices over an encrypted WebRTC DataChannel — the server never sees the content.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Display your name inside rooms
  • Allow you to rejoin rooms after a brief disconnection

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing of any kind.

03 Social Sign-In (Google & Facebook)

When you choose to sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive from that provider:

  • Your name as it appears on the platform
  • Your email address (if shared by the platform)
  • Your public profile picture URL

We do not receive your password, friends list, posts, messages, or any other account data from these platforms.

Account linking: If you sign in with Google or Facebook using the same email address as an existing email/password account, MuDi will link them to the same account automatically.

Social sign-in is governed by the respective platform's privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Data Policy

04 Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored in a SQLite database on the server hosting this application. We take the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12) — they are never stored in plaintext
  • All connections are encrypted via HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • WebRTC audio transfers use DTLS-SRTP end-to-end encryption
  • Session cookies are marked HttpOnly and Secure

No security system is perfect. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your MuDi account.

05 Cookies & Sessions

MuDi uses a single session cookie to keep you signed in. This cookie:

  • Is named connect.sid
  • Is set when you sign in and expires after 7 days
  • Contains only a session identifier — no personal data
  • Is marked HttpOnly (not accessible to JavaScript) and Secure (HTTPS only)

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

06 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Your display name is shared with other users in the same room. Your email address is never shown to other users.

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of users.

07 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact the app administrator. We will respond within 30 days.

08 Children's Privacy

MuDi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of MuDi after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
